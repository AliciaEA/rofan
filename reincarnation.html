Reincarnation Cliche

As many of the other genres, reincarnation has its own cliches. We have for example, the 'Oh I read a novel and died, and now I reincarnated and have to save my life'

But let me explain, reincarnation is not the same as transmigration. When we talk about reincarnation, is the fact that the female lead was born there, but has the memories of her past life. However, Transmigration is possesing someone else's body, like stealing his mind and body and now it is yours.

Reincarnation per se, is a cliche. And obviously, the cliche depends on the other genres of the novel or manhwa. But the one that I'm talking about is Romance + Reincarnation
